how TikTok's algorithm led to the annulment of a presidential election

At the end of 2024, Romanians across the globe were voting for their next president. In the first round of the 2 round voting process, there were more than 10 candidates including independent candidate Calin Georgescu.
A week later, the Constitutional Court annulled the election due to foreign interference
and the manipulation of TikTok's algorithm in Georgescu's favour.

How did this happen?

What is Algorithmic Bias?

Algorithm bias is an event which occurs when an algorithm produces unfair or biased results.

In a political context, it can cause political polarisation, which is when politically-balanced users are pushed to extremism, either left or right. Algorithms are made to promote what's popular and that can sometime fuel political polarisaiton and division.
